Here are some tips to find the right programming language for you:

Ask yourself this...

  1. What interests me about coding?
  2. What am I passionate about outside of coding?
  3. What kind of teams do I work best with? What kind of team-player am I?

Once you ask yourself these questions, you can start to become more familiar with what type of programmer you want to be.
